# ⚖️ ND's BMI Coach for All

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/demo-live-brightgreen)](https://nd-bmi-coach.lovable.app)
[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](LICENSE)

A smart, interactive BMI calculator that goes beyond the numbers — it provides **personalised wellness advice** based on your BMI category and daily routines.

👉 **Try it live:** [https://nd-bmi-coach.lovable.app](https://nd-bmi-coach.lovable.app)

---

## ✨ Features

- **Instant BMI calculation** – Enter your height and weight (any units) and get your BMI and category (underweight / normal / overweight) in real time.
- **Two intelligent paths:**
  - **Normal BMI** → Asks 8 simple questions about your daily routines → generates **personalised suggestions** to help you maintain or improve your health.
  - **Underweight / Overweight** → Takes your age → automatically provides a complete wellness package including **general advice, diet plan, exercise routine, and sleep tips**.
- **No prompt engineering required** – The logic is built‑in; you just answer simple questions.
- **Privacy‑first** – All calculations happen on the client side; no data is stored or shared.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. **Input** your height and weight (feet/inches or cm, kg/lbs – works with any unit).
2. The app calculates your **BMI** and instantly shows your category.
3. **Depending on your category:**
   - **Normal BMI** → You'll answer 8 quick questions about your daily habits (e.g., sleep, water intake, physical activity). Based on your answers, the app crafts **personalised tips** to keep you on track.
   - **Extreme BMI** (underweight or overweight) → You'll enter your age, and the app generates a **customised plan** covering diet, exercise, and sleep – all tailored to help you reach a healthier range.
